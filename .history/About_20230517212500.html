About Us

The CETL fosters the values and practices of excellent teaching and learning for students learning and staffs professional development. CETL endeavors to be a professional and lifelong learning center to facilitate andragogy inspired teaching and learning; to effectively apply and integrate e-learning or electronic technologies for online, distance, virtual and blended modes of education; to train faculty for 21st century teaching and learning, to prepare staff for adaptation to interactive digital media development, usage, and application; to administer and enhance the universitys Learning Management System and to prepare, assist in deployment and innovate emerging technologies through continuous renewal.
