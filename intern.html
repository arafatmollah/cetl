The Green University Center of Excellence for Teaching And Learning refers to a team, a shared facility or an entity that provides leadership, evangelization, best practices, research, support and/or training for a focus area in the teaching and learning contexts. CETL also aims at revitalizing stalled initiatives. Within the university, CETL refers to a group of people, a department or a shared facility. It also is known as a competency center or a capability center. The term also refers to a network of institutions collaborating with each other to pursue excellence in a particular academic, research and/or training area.
